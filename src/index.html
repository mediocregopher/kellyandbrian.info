We Eloped!

On Monday, February 22, 2021, Brian Picciano and Kelly Dutton, with just a photographer present to witness, self-solemnized their marriage in Evergreen, Colorado.

After a short hike to the middle of a snowy aspen grove off Beaver Creek Trail, we said our self-written vows to each other, and finally became husband and wife.

The rest of the afternoon was spent traipsing around the forest, taking pictures in the snow, laughing, crying, freezing, and, above all, being endlessly grateful for each other.
